Newsfeed Analysis

Exposure Analytics using Newsfeeds

Challenges
  • Insurance companies want to reduce exposure to financial risks due to catastrophe events
  • Underwriters require information on likely occurrence of catastrophe events to assess potential losses and thereby take preventive measures to minimize losses
  • It is required to estimate approximate number of expected claims from each catastrophe hit region and total claim amount
    		•
    Solution
  • Uses web scraping to extract News data from Web
  • AI and Machine Learning (ML) provides insights in an easily consumable format
  • Uses various unsupervised ML techniques to generate insights and inferences from the data such as region with higher claims etc.
  • Creates interactive visualizations from the data such as network graph to show relation between events and organizations
    •  
    Benefits
  • Estimation of potential losses due to catastrophe
  • Location level risk exposure helps take better loss control measures. Improves profitability. It gives approximate number of claims that will come from a specific area and potential losses pertaining to it
  • Automated insights offer timely information for precautionary actions to be taken
  • Sentiment analysis on the organizations mentioned in news articles helps to closely monitor organizations with negative sentiments
    		•
    Capabilities & Technology
  • Understanding news data in text form using NLP and Text Analytics
  • Information extraction
  • Sentiment analysis
  • Text data clustering and classifications
  • Technology - Python, Flask, plotly
